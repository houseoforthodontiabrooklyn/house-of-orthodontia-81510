Frequently Asked Questions About Metal Braces

How much do metal braces typically cost at House of Orthodontia in Brooklyn, and do you offer payment plans?
At House of Orthodontia, the total cost for metal braces usually ranges from $4,000 to $6,500, depending on the complexity of your case and length of treatment. We understand that orthodontic care is a significant investment, so we offer flexible, interest-free payment plans to help make treatment more affordable. Our team will review all costs upfront during your consultation and work with you to find a payment option that fits your budget. We also accept most major dental insurance plans.
How long will I need to wear metal braces if I start treatment at your Brooklyn office?
The average treatment time for metal braces at House of Orthodontia is between 18 and 24 months, though it can vary based on your specific needs. Factors like the severity of misalignment, your age, and how well you follow care instructions can affect the timeline. During your first visit, our orthodontists will provide a customized estimate after examining your teeth and discussing your goals.
Are your orthodontists licensed and experienced in providing metal braces in New York?
Yes, all orthodontists at House of Orthodontia are fully licensed by the New York State Education Department and are members of the American Association of Orthodontists. Our team has extensive experience in fitting and managing metal braces for patients of all ages in Brooklyn and throughout New York City.
What is the process for getting metal braces at House of Orthodontia, from consultation to placement?
Your journey begins with a comprehensive consultation at our Brooklyn office, where we take digital x-rays, photos, and impressions to assess your smile. Our orthodontist will then discuss your treatment options and create a personalized plan. On the day of placement, we clean your teeth, bond the brackets, and place the archwire—this visit usually takes about 1–2 hours. You'll then return for monthly adjustment appointments until your treatment is complete.
Do you offer any guarantees or warranties on your metal braces treatment?
House of Orthodontia stands behind the quality of our care. We provide complimentary repairs for issues like broken brackets or wires during your active treatment, as long as they result from normal wear. After your braces are removed, we include a set of retainers and provide guidance on maintaining your results. While long-term success depends on proper retainer use, our team is always available for follow-up support in our Brooklyn office.
